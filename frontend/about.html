System Overview

The Factory Machinery Status & Repair Tracking System helps monitor the health of machines in a car manufacturing facility. It provides real-time updates, fault tracking, and repair management to reduce downtime and improve productivity.

Purpose

Our system is built to streamline maintenance workflows. From identifying faults to assigning technicians and monitoring progress, everything is centralized in one intuitive platform.

Key Benefits

  • Live machine status updates
  • Faster fault reporting and resolution
  • Improved team coordination
  • Access to complete maintenance history
